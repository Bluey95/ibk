What We Do

Entrepreneurship training

We equip micro-entrepreneurs using a mix of enterprise training using our Kenyan based business curriculum, consultancy support and responsible finance. Our 3-month training acts as collateral to start up capital for youth and entrepreneurs.

TechMtaani Digital Computing Skills

TechMtaani is a project that aims at assisting youth exploit the nexus between Technology and Entrepreneurship. Computing being an integral part of everyday life in the 21st century has changed the way we work and live. One of the basic skill in the job market is to have computing skills.

Pitch Weekend
